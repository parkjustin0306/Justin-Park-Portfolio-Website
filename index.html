About Me

My name is Justin, and I'm constantly driven by a curiosity to understand the 'why' behind user behavior, which fuels my passion to learn more about these fields every day. I enjoy creating user-friendly and visually appealing digital experiences that prioritize the needs and preferences of users. My interests lie in UI/UX Design, Human Centered Design, and Website Development and Designing.

I’m a second-year student at Rochester Institute of Technology focused on UI/UX design and human-centered computing. Through coursework and hands-on projects, I explore how thoughtful design, accessibility, and interaction principles shape meaningful digital experiences. This portfolio highlights both individual and collaborative projects that reflect my approach to designing intuitive, inclusive, and user-focused interfaces. I am especially interested in co-ops and internships where I can contribute to creating technology that prioritizes usability, clarity, and real human needs.
